David Hayter

David Hayter has been the voice actor of Solid Snake and Naked Snake for all of the game series until the very last game in the series Metal Gear Solid 5 in which Hideo Kojima wanted a change for the series because of the aging of the character and series. David is also a very big screen writer some of his work can be shown in the movies Watchmen, X-Men series, and many more. David has personally completed the game series unlike some actors who have only played one of the games.

Quinton Flynn

Quinton Flynn is known for his various voice acting skills across many shows and video games. Some of his work can be shown in Naruto: the orignal series, One Piece, Metal Gear Solid Series , and etc. Quinton Flynn voice acts as Raiden in the Metal Gear Solid Series. He is apart of the series all the way up to Metal Gear Rising which is a spin off of the main game in which it primarly focuses on Raiden and his path.

Christopher Randolph

Christopher Randolph voice acts as the character Octacon in the series. Christopher Randolph has been in the series since Metal Gear Solid which was made in 1998. Christopher Randolph is very flexible with his work capabilities such as he is capable of doing writing, directing, and teaching acting. He also voice acts Dr. Huey Emmerich who is Octacon's father.

Kiefer Sutherland

Kiefer is part of many television shows and movies. He is knownfor his roles in 24, Touch, Stand By Me, The Lost Boys, Flatliners, Phone Booth, etc. Kiefer did facial motion cature and the english voices for Big Boss/Venom Snake in Metal Gear Solid 5: Ground Zero/The Phantom Pain. Sutherland has earned himself over the years numeerous awards such as an Emmy Award, Golden Globe Award, Screen Actors Guild Awards, and Satellite Awards.
